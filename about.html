Hello! It's nice to meet you. My name's Abby, and I'm a rising senior at the University of Pittsburgh.

I believe that design and user-centered practices are going to take front stage in the coming years. As technology becomes increasingly blurred with reality, I want nothing more than to contribute to the interfaces shaping our future.

When I'm offline, I like to read philosophy books that go way over my head and check out concerts in my local music scene. I'm a huge fan of yoga, a good London Fog, and (evidently) every single cat I come across.
