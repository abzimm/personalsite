Open Lab

Overview

Open Lab is a free-to-use makerspace on the University of Pittsburgh’s campus. It offers a variety of fabrication equipment and workshops to students, faculty, and staff. I was asked to redesign the website to make it more user friendly, as members frequently had trouble navigating through it.

Time

1 week

Tools

Figma
Airtable

Deliverable

Design file to hand off to Pitt IT developers

Context

The main purpose of the Open Lab website is for people to learn about how to become a member. Instead, students and faculty were coming into the physical location to ask staff directly. The common opener was: “I went to the website, but...”

Myself, the head of Open Lab, and five other staff members sat down to review the problems. Eventually, we outlined two main goals.

  • Differentiate student and faculty needs

  • Remove bloat and outdated information

With these insights, I then focused on specific aspects of the website.
